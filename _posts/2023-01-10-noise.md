Signal integrity lives in the white space of the schematic


## Signal integrity
noise problems associated with the signals that propagate through the interconnects.
- Reflection noise
- Terminations and routing topologies
- Cross talk
- Attenuation and loss
- Mode conversions


## Power integrity
noise problems associated with the delivery of the nominally constant DC voltage to each active device, usually caused by power and return currents.
- Core logic voltage noise
- On-die capacitance
- Bandini mountains
- Package power path loop inductance
- On-package capacitors
- Decoupling capacitor integration and selection
